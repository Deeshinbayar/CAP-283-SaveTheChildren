About Us

Save the Children is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of children around the world.
With your support, we provide healthcare, education, and emergency relief to children in need.


Our mission is to inspire breakthroughs in the way the world treats children and to achieve immediate and lasting change in their lives.

Through our programs, we provide:

Quality Education: We establish schools, provide educational resources, and promote education as a fundamental right for every child.
Essential Healthcare: We ensure access to healthcare services, vaccinations, and nutrition, combating diseases and improving child health.
Emergency Response: We respond swiftly during crises, offering life-saving assistance, clean water, shelter, and psychological support to affected children and families.
Advocacy and Awareness: We advocate for children's rights, raise awareness about issues affecting children, and work with governments and communities to create positive change.

Our Programs

  • Education: Providing access to quality education and educational resources.
  • Healthcare: Ensuring access to essential healthcare services and medical assistance.
  • Emergency Response: Rapid response during crises, offering relief and support to affected children and families.
